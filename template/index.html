About Me

I currently reside in Raleigh, North Carolina, the place I call home since moving here back in 2000 with my family. I have two self-less, awesome parents and one younger brother, Shaun. Looking to build my skill sets and further my educational knowledge, I recently graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill - Full-Stack Web Development program.
